Areas of Expertise

Areas of Expertise

Head and Neck Cancer

A group of cancers of the mouth, sinuses, nose or throat.

Skull Base Cancer

Skull base tumors are not a particular type of brain tumor but those that grow in a particular location: the bones of the skull that form the bottom of the head and the bony ridge behind the nose and eyes.

Thyroid Cancer

A cancer of the thyroid, the butterfly-shaped gland at the base of the neck.

Reconstructive Surgery

Reconstruction of the head and neck is often an important part of treatment for head and neck cancer. It is aimed at restoring the function and appearance of the face, mouth, neck, and throat.

Micro Vascular Surgery

Head and neck cancer surgery, which involves the removal of tumors and delicate tissues, can impact the cosmetic form, look and function of facial structures. These surgeries restore your appearance and quality of life following treatment.
